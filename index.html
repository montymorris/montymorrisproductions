I am a passionate sound engineer and lighting designer with a strong background in live performance. I have mixed sound and designed lighting for musicals, bands, dance shows and theatre productions, as well as recorded orchestras, choirs, and rock bands. I thrive under pressure and value collaboration with directors, performers, and tech crews.

I attend the theatre and the opera, constantly learning from productions and industry professionals. My freelance work, gap year technician role, and mentorship of others reflect my commitment to technical theatre, while my active musical life as a tenor within National Youth Choir enhances my understanding of live performance.

I am currently studying BA (Hons) Stage Management and Technical Theatre at RWCMD.
